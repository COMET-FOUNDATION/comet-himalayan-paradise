# CHP Himalayan Paradise – Holiday Camp & Himalayan Trek Programs

## Overview

CHP Himalayan Paradise is a travel and adventure destination focused on providing immersive experiences in the Himalayan region. It combines comfortable accommodation, curated holiday camps, nature-based activities, wellness programs, and guided trekking experiences to create customizable travel packages for individuals, families, groups, and adventure enthusiasts.

The platform offers both **on-campus recreational activities** and **off-campus Himalayan expeditions**, enabling visitors to choose experiences ranging from a single-day outing to multi-day adventure programs.
